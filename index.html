About US

Porsche Aktiengesellschaft, usually abbreviated as Porsche AG,4 is a German automobile manufacturer specializing in high-end luxury sports cars, sport utility vehicles (SUVs) and sedans. It is owned by the Volkswagen Group,5 which in turn is majority owned by Porsche Automobil Holding SE. It was founded in Stuttgart in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche and his son Ferdinand Anton Ernst Porsche.

Tracks

The new Porsche 911 GT3 RS completed the 20 kilometers and 810 meters of the Nürburgring Nordschleife circuit in 6:49.328 minutes, 10.6 seconds faster than the time achieved by the 911 GT3. At the wheel of the new model was Porsche ambassador and former driver Jörg Bergmeister, who has been actively involved in the development of the vehicle.
